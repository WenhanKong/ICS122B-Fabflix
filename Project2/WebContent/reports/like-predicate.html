I used the most simple pattern LIKE which is provided by mysql.

for movie title, I used LIKE %movie_name% to predict;

for movie year, I used LIKE 'year';

for movie director, I used LIKE %director%;

for name, I split name into two firstname and lastname, then use "name like '%full_name[0]%' OR s.name like '% full_name[1] %'" to predict;
